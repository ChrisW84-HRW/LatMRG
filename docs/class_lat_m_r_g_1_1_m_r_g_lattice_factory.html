Creates a MRGLattice from the combination of \(J\) MRGComponent’s, with maximal dimension maxDim, lacunary indices Lac, lattice type lat and vector norm norm.

Lac can be NULL if no lacunary indices are to be used. The combined MRG is calculated as described in [rLEC96b].

Creates a MRGLattice from a multiply-with-carry (MWC) recurrence, with maximal dimension maxDim, lacunary indices Lac, lattice type lat and vector norm norm.

The MWC recurrence is

\begin{align*} x_n & = (a_1 x_{n-1} + \cdots+ a_k x_{n-k} + c_{n-1})d \mbox{ mod } b, \\ c_n & = \lfloor(a_0 x_n + a_1 x_{n-1} +\cdots+ a_k x_{n-k} + c_{n-1}) / b \rfloor \end{align*}

where \(b\) is a positive integer, \(a_0,\cdots,a_k\) are arbitrary integers such that \(a_0\) is relatively prime to \(b\), and \(d\) is the multiplicative inverse of \(-a_0\) mod \(b\). The MRG derived from such a MWC is defined by \( m = \sum^k_{l=0} a_l b^l \) and \(a\) is the inverse of \(b\) in arithmetic modulo \(m\).
